Guidelines for handling user inquiries, tagging teams, and follow-up processes.

Summary

The CS Team SOP focuses on managing user inquiries, escalating to relevant teams when necessary, and ensuring efficient responses to user issues.

Key Advice

  • Tag the correct team based on the inquiry type.
  • Provide clear and concise information to users.
  • Follow up on escalated cases to ensure resolution.
