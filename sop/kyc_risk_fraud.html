Guidelines for identifying, investigating, and handling high-risk activities with the KYC, Risk, and Fraud teams.

Summary

This SOP outlines the responsibilities of the KYC, Risk, and Fraud teams in handling high-risk activities and suspicious accounts.

Key Advice

  • Collaborate closely with each team to identify suspicious behavior.
  • Document findings and actions taken for transparency.
  • Follow escalation protocols for unresolved cases.
