Guidelines for managing user transactions, monitoring for fraud, and handling deposit/withdrawal issues.

Summary

The Finance Team SOP focuses on overseeing user transactions, addressing deposit and withdrawal inquiries, and identifying suspicious account behaviors.

Key Advice

  • Verify transaction details before taking action.
  • Clearly communicate with users, especially for irrecoverable issues.
  • Escalate cases with multiple-account evidence to the KYC team.
